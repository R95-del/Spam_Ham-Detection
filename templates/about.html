About the Topic and reason behind taking the project.

Client based Anti Spam Detection is a topic which is choosen because The businesss are growing online at very huge rate where there comunication is done by the means of email, sms as well feedback or comments And this is area where online fraud and phishing customer takes place through various means in which spam content through email sms are biggest thing so, to avoid phising and spam content in communication and get customer confidence on recieved sms or email from different sources we have taken a particular project.

Our Goal

The main objective of Project is to develop a Text classification Model through various avialable machine learning and NLP techniques. The primary focus of the indigenously developed text classification model is to automatically analyze and categorize content in email, sms and comment is SPAM/HAM which will in turn help many service provider company. With the increase in email and sms manually checking and classifying the content is impossible task. the model is very much usefull.
